How to Apply for our Hostels?

To apply for hostel accommodation at Sabaragamuwa University of Sri Lanka, students should submit their application during the registration process. This procedure is overseen by the Student Affairs Department. Eligibility for hostel facilities is granted to students residing more than 40 kilometers from the university. Upon enrollment, the university will allocate a hostel to each eligible student, facilitating their transition to academic life on campus.
